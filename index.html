Kentucky Stroke Mortality & Access to Stroke-Certified Hospitals

Full-screen map

Authored by: Jeff Levy, a student of New Maps Plus

This map was produced in CARTO, with layers from my MPH Capstone Defense paper, An Ecologic Study of Access to Acute Stroke Care, Mortality and Its Correlates in the Stroke Belt. Datasets are originally acquired from the Interactive Atlas of Heart Disease and Stroke, and route information generated with ESRI Business Analyst road data. Only hospitals certified as "Comprehensive Stroke Centers", "Primary Stroke Centers", or "Acute Stroke Ready Hospitals" by the Joint Commission as of April 1, 2017 were considered for this study. The map uses the Web Mercator (EPSG:3857) coordinate system.
